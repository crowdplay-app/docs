---
title: Quick Start
description: Get up and running with CrowdPlay in under 5 minutes.
---

## For DJs

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an Account">
    Go to the [DJ Dashboard](https://crowdplay-api-i1xh.onrender.com/login) and click **Register**. Enter a username and password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a Session">
    From your dashboard, click **Create New Session**. Give it a name (e.g. "Friday Night Party"). You can create up to **20 sessions**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share the QR Code">
    Click on your session to open it. You'll see a **QR code** — display it on a screen or share it with your crowd. Fans scan it to join.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage the Queue">
    As fans submit requests, they appear in your queue in real-time. Use the controls to:
    - **Play Next** — move a song to "Now Playing"
    - **Skip** — skip the current song
    - **Reorder** — move songs up or down in the queue
    - **Remove** — delete a song from the queue
  </Step>
</Steps>

## For Fans

<Steps>
  <Step title="Scan the QR Code">
    Use your phone camera to scan the QR code displayed by the DJ. This opens the CrowdPlay fan client.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Nickname">
    Type a nickname so the DJ knows who requested the song.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search & Request">
    Search for any song using the search bar (powered by Deezer). Tap a result to submit your request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upvote">
    If someone already requested a song you want, it gets automatically upvoted. Popular songs rise to the top!
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Each fan can only upvote a specific song **once** per session to keep things fair.
</Info>
